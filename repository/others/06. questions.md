## 项目成功的经验

### 中文

### English

I’ve found that projects go much smoother when you spend enough time on research and planning upfront. Before coding, I like to study the requirements, explore technologies, and create a detailed technical plan. This helps the team avoid mistakes later and work more efficiently.
